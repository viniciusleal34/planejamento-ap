## Como Usar Estes Prompts

Copie os prompts abaixo e cole diretamente em:
- **Midjourney** (Discord ou web app) — adicione `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` ao final
- **DALL-E 3** (ChatGPT Plus) — cole o prompt diretamente
- **Adobe Firefly** — funciona bem com os prompts em inglês
- **Stable Diffusion** — use os prompts como positive prompt

> Todos os prompts são em **inglês** para máxima compatibilidade com ferramentas de IA.

## 🛋️ Prompt 1 — Sala Integrada com Cozinha (Vista Geral)

```
Hyperrealistic architectural interior render of a modern contemporary open-plan living room 
integrated with kitchen in a compact 44sqm Brazilian apartment. Style: minimalist, clean, 
sophisticated. Color palette: off-white walls (#F5F0EB), light grey cement-look porcelain 
floor tiles 60x120cm, matte black accents.

Key elements: large black matte retractable sofa 180cm as the main focal point, white inverse 
refrigerator integrated with white lacquered kitchen cabinets, ribbed wood panel wall behind TV 
with warm LED backlight, minimalist floating TV rack with black matte finish, light grey neutral 
rug 150x200cm under the sofa, sheer linen white curtains floor-to-ceiling.

Lighting: soft natural daylight entering through large windows, warm 3000K indirect LED lighting 
in plaster ceiling cove, 3 directional spots on black aluminum track above sofa, pendant globe 
light above coffee table. Plaster ceiling with modern recessed lighting design.

Perspective: wide angle 24mm lens, eye level 120cm, slight upward tilt. High definition, 
professional architecture photography quality, photorealistic, 8K, award-winning interior design.
```

**Parâmetros Midjourney sugeridos**: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw --q 2`
