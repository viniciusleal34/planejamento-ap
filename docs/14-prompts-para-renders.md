## 🛋️ Prompt 1 — Sala Integrada com Cozinha (Vista Geral)

```
Hyper-realistic interior design render of a compact modern apartment, open-plan living room and kitchen, 44 square meters. Wide-angle perspective. A black 180cm retractable sofa as the centerpiece facing a minimalist TV wall panel combining light oak wood and black shelves. Large decorative mirror with thin black frame on the side wall reflecting natural light. Light gray polypropylene rug on the floor. Sheer white linen curtains. The kitchen features white flat-panel cabinets with light wood accents, black granite countertop, and a white bottom-freezer refrigerator. Continuous cement-look porcelain tile flooring throughout. Recessed ceiling with LED strip lighting in warm 3000K. Soft natural light coming through large windows. Color palette: white walls, light gray, black accents, light oak wood. Clean, sophisticated, minimalist Scandinavian-modern style. Architectural photography quality, 8K resolution, photorealistic.
```

## 🍳 Prompt 2 — Cozinha

```
Hyper-realistic interior render of a compact modern kitchen in a Brazilian apartment. White flat-panel MDF cabinets with light oak wood detail strip and hidden handles. Black São Gabriel granite countertop with undermount stainless steel sink and matte black single-lever faucet. White bottom-freezer inverse refrigerator. Black glass 4-burner cooktop. LED strip lighting under upper cabinets in warm 3000K. Cement-look porcelain tile floor. White walls. Recessed ceiling with spotlights. Clean, organized, minimalist. Wide-angle lens, soft natural lighting, architectural photography quality, 8K, photorealistic.
```

## 🌿 Prompt 7 — Varanda

## 💡 Dicas para Melhores Resultados

- No **Midjourney**: adicione `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --s 750` ao final
- No **DALL-E/ChatGPT**: use como está, pode pedir ajustes depois
- No **Leonardo AI**: selecione modelo "PhotoReal" ou "Absolute Reality"
- Gere múltiplas variações e escolha a melhor
